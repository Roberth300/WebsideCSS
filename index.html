Our story starts under a different name: The Best Buy Children’s Foundation set its early sights on supporting K-12 education, life skills, health and wellness and leadership development for children of all ages. The Foundation launched in line with the first annual Best Buy Foundation Charity Classic event, then just a one-day golf tournament but already rooted on a bedrock of partnership and collaboration.

our services our social impact

Geek Squad Academy

Technology can open up new opportunities for youth, both in their lives today and their future careers. Geek Squad Academy gives “Junior Agents” hands-on experience with technology so they can open new doors, and Geek Squad Academy Online Learning provides free courses on STEM subjects like coding, 3D design, and more..

School Tech Grants

Guided by the principle of better education through better technology, the Best Buy School Tech Grants program exists to equip Canadian students with the technology they need to keep them inspired, motivated, and empowered as they learn and grow.

Partnership with BGC Canada

Best Buy Canada has been a proud supporter of the BGC Canada (formerly Boys and Girls Clubs of Canada) since 2001. We’ve donated more than $3 million in scholarships, and our stores partner with Boys and Girls Clubs across the country to find ways to give back. We are also proud to help fund BGC Canada’s Anti-Racism Initiative Microgrants (AIM), which aim to support youth-led, anti-racism initiatives from Club members across Canada.

Teen Tech Centres

In 2021, Best Buy Canada launched its first Teen Tech Centre – a free, safe, and creative place to do hands-on learning with professional level tech. Participating teens learn new skills, stay on track with school, and nurture positive peer relationships – which could help open doors for a future career.

A culture of giving back

Our employee-supported program, Blue Gives Back, encourages Best Buy employees to volunteer in the communities where we live and work, as well as matches employee’s monetary donations. We believe change starts with us, so we’re dedicated to contributing to a culture of giving and volunteering.

Building a stronger, more inclusive and diverse tech industry

At Best Buy, we want to help underrepresented tech entrepreneurs achieve their goals. That's why we've launched our Mentorship & Accelerator Program to connect underrepresented innovators with the tools to help grow their business.
